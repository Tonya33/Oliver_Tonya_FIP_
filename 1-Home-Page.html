RAW Stone uses Stainless steel chain and fixings to create one of a kind pieces. Stainless steel is made with up to 10% chormium, proventing the Stainless Steel from tarnihing. Although, If the Stainless Steel chain is exposed to salt water, it could strip away the chromium-making way for tarnish to occur. So if you're thinking of hitting the beach, best to leave your RAW STONE in your purse!
& Remember- nothing is set in stone! If your Jewelry is exposed to moisture for too long, it will eventually tarnish!

Storing: dry, moisture free
Cleaning: Water and soft hand soap
Make sure the jewelry piece is 100% dry after cleaning!
